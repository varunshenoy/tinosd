Marty De

Head Coach

Marty De is the Director of Speech and Debate at Cupertino High School and will be the lead instructor for the camp. He has coached tournament champions in Lincoln Douglas Debate, Public Forum Debate, and Congressional Debate and has 7 years of coaching experience. As a coach in the National Speech and Debate Association, he has earned the degree of One Diamond and has been selected to judge national final rounds of debate. He has coached Cupertino High School to numerous sweepstakes awards, and currently has a team of over 100 members.
